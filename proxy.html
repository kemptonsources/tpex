A proxy ordinance is an ordinance where a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints does the ordinances inplace of a person who has died.

baptistry explanation. The Baptistry is where members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints are baptized for those people who have died

Endowment explanation. The Endowment also known as blessing is where members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints make promises, acting in the stead of those who have died, to God that they will keep certain laws of God.

Sealing room explanation. The Sealing room is where a man and a woman are sealed together on behalf of a man and woman who died. Being sealed means that man and woman will be married in the life to come.
